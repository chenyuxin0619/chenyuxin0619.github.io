About Me

I am an assistant professor at the analytics and operations group of Imperial College Business School from January 2021. I received my Ph.D. from Department of Management Science and Engineering at Stanford, advised by Prof. Kay Giesecke, Prof. Markus Pelger, and Prof. Yinyu Ye. Before coming to Stanford, I obtained my B.S. degree from School of Mathematical Sciences at Peking University in 2014.

More about my research can be found on my research page.
